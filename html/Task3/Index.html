Introduction

The Argentina national football team is one of the most iconic teams in FIFA World Cup history, known for its passion, skill, and legendary players.

History

Argentina has won the FIFA World Cup three times (1978, 1986, 2022). The team has produced some of the greatest footballers ever, including Diego Maradona and Lionel Messi.
